payment_type codes (how the passenger paid):
1 = Credit card
2 = Cash
3 = No charge
4 = Dispute
5 = Unknown
6 = Voided trip
Use payment_type when filtering by payment method.

trip_distance is measured in miles from the taximeter.
Short trips are under 1 mile. Long trips are over 10 miles.
Average trip distance in 2024 is approximately 3.2 miles.

fare_amount is the time-and-distance fare calculated by the meter.
It does not include tips, tolls, or surcharges.
total_amount includes all charges: fare, tip, tolls, surcharges, and taxes.
tip_amount is only recorded for credit card payments. Cash tips are not captured.

RatecodeID defines the fare rate type applied for the trip:
1 = Standard rate (most trips)
2 = JFK airport flat rate ($70 + tolls)
3 = Newark airport
4 = Nassau or Westchester
5 = Negotiated fare (pre-arranged price, not metered)
6 = Group ride
Use RatecodeID=2 to filter for JFK airport trips.
Use RatecodeID=5 to filter for negotiated fare trips.

PULocationID and DOLocationID are NYC taxi zone IDs.
Manhattan zones are roughly 1–105.
Airport zones: JFK = 132, LaGuardia = 138, Newark = 1.
Use these IDs when filtering trips by pickup or dropoff location.

tpep_pickup_datetime and tpep_dropoff_datetime are timestamps.
Use them to filter by hour of day, day of week, or date range.
Peak hours are 7–9 AM and 5–8 PM on weekdays.
